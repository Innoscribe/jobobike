# KeyFeatures Standardization Guide

## Standard Format (Remove brand names, keep only technical specs):

1. Motor: "250W, XXNm" (remove XOFO, Bafang, etc.)
2. Battery: "XXV XXAh (XXXWh)" (remove Samsung, LG, etc.)
3. Range: "Opptil XX km rekkevidde"
4. Tires: "XX×X.X dekk" (remove CST, Kenda, etc.)
5. Frame: "6061 Aluminiumslegering ramme" OR "Høyfast stål ramme"
6. Suspension: "Fjærgaffel demping" OR "Full demping" (if applicable)
7. Brakes: "Hydrauliske skivebremser" OR "Mekaniske skivebremser" (remove Tektro)
8. Drivetrain: "Shimano X-Speed (X gir)" OR "Single Speed (1 gir)"
9. Folding: "Kompakt sammenlegging XX×XX×XXcm" (if foldable)
10. Display: "Color LCD" OR "LCD" OR "LED" (remove brand names, Bluetooth mentions)
11. Size: "En størrelse"

## Remove:
- Brand names (XOFO, Bafang, Samsung, LG, Tektro, CST, Kenda, YL-81F)
- Extra descriptors (Maks last XXkg, ca. 100L, m/USB, + Bluetooth)
- Accessory mentions (Integrert lastekasse, Bakre bagasjebrett, Stor bak-kurv)
